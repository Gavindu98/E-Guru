E-GURU is for not only students but also teachers. E-GURU will let you sign up as either a teacher or a student. You can create lessons for students as a teacher and both can participate in the question forum, study the lessons, and take quizzes.

How to be a teacher on E-GURU

You must SIGN UP by selecting the Teacher option. then you can create lessons, quizzes, and add e-books to the library. You can participate in the question forum as a teacher as well. You may submit questions and answers.

How to be a student on E-GURU

If you wish to be a student, you must select the Student option while registering. then you can make any necessary searches for lessons based on the subject and your grade. The quiz section is for you in particular. also, you can refer our e-library. You can participate in the question forum by posting your questions and answers.
